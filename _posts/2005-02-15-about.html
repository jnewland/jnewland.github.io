What is this?

This is the personal weblog/web-design sandbox of Jesse Newland. I am constantly tweaking the design, posting links from sites I read, and writing articles about the web, technology, politics, and Jager Bombs.

What should I check out?

The "In The News" block to your left is a auto-updating list of links that I found interesting. I post these rather regularly: from one to ten a day. Basically, read these if you want to be able to strike up an interesting conversation with me.

The posts below are irregular rants about whatever is on my mind.

If you're as addicted to the RSS phenomenon as I am, you might be interested in my RSS feeds (blog, "in the news" links) or my OPML.

Who are you?

I'm Jesse Newland, a 21 year old student living in Athens, GA while attending the University of Georgia. I'm majoring in Management of Information Systems and am currently employed by the School of Social work as a Tech Support Staff member/Sysadmin/Linux Guru/Web Designer.
